Features

Data-Driven Visualization

Create cartograms where region sizes reflect your data values, or build choropleth maps with color-coded statistical information.

Flexible Map Formats

Upload your custom geographic data (GeoJSON/Shapefile) or choose from our library.

Custom Data Editing

Upload CSV/Excel files or use our built-in editor to quickly manage your data.

Smart Color Palettes

Topology-aware coloring ensures adjacent regions have distinct colors. Choose from preset palettes or create your own custom schemes.

Inset Maps & Labels

Better visualize specific regions with inset maps. Region labels are automatically placed and scaled according to area size for optimal readability.

Share & Embed

Export your maps as SVG or JSON files, share via direct links, or embed visualizations in websites with responsive iframe support.

Privacy Options

Use our online service for convenience, or deploy offline via Docker/CLI for complete data privacy. Your choice, your data.

Free & Open Source

Completely free to use with full source code available. Community-driven development with no hidden costs or subscriptions.
